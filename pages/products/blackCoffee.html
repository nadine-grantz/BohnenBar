Black Coffee - Single Origin

Origin: Colombia | Huila | Castillo

Tasting Notes: Dark Chocolate | Caramel | Red Apple

Black Coffee from the Huila region of Colombia is a testament to the rich and bold flavors characteristic of this renowned coffee-growing area. The Castillo beans deliver a robust dark chocolate body, accented with caramel sweetness and a hint of red apple acidity. This single-origin coffee is ideal for those who appreciate a full-bodied cup with a complex flavor profile, perfect for both drip brewing and espresso.
