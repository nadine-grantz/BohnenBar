Aurora Blend - Direct Trade

Origin: Ethiopia | Sidamo | Heirloom Varieties

Tasting Notes: Blueberry | Lemon | Honey

The Aurora Blend is a remarkable coffee that shines with vibrant and fruity flavors. Sourced from the fertile highlands of Sidamo, Ethiopia, this blend features heirloom varieties cultivated with meticulous care. The region's unique terroir imparts a distinct blueberry and lemon profile, complemented by a smooth honey finish. Perfectly balanced, the Aurora Blend offers a delightful experience whether enjoyed as a pour-over or a bright espresso.
