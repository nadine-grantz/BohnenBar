Monte Verde - Single Origin

Origin: Costa Rica | Tarrazú | Caturra

Tasting Notes: Citrus | Brown Sugar | Hazelnut

Monte Verde hails from the picturesque Tarrazú region of Costa Rica, where the Caturra beans are grown under ideal conditions. This coffee features a lively citrus acidity, balanced by the sweetness of brown sugar and a rich hazelnut undertone. Renowned for its clarity and brightness, Monte Verde is an excellent choice for pour-over and filter methods, bringing a taste of Costa Rica’s lush landscapes to your cup.
